The Ultimate Summer Jewelry Guide from a Celebrity Stylist


This summer, jewelry is all about making a statement: chic gold earrings, timeless watches, large molten bangles. When looking through the treasures of your jewelry box, you want your collection of gold gems and beauties precisely selected. Yes, we all want to be wearing the latest and greatest this summer season, but we also want to be able to wear these dazzling pieces year-round. So we asked Dani Michelle, celebrity stylist and the creative force behind the looks of Hailey Bieber, Kendall Jenner, and Daisy Edgar-Jones, to share her insider knowledge on the must-have pieces of the season. Spoiler alert: mixed metals and pearls are in...

Combining jewellery correctly:

There are hardly any rules when it comes to combining jewellery, almost everything is allowed. The general rule is: creativity and joy are the be-all and end-all. If you like to wear a lot of jewellery, you can also combine many pieces of jewellery with each other. The only rule of thumb is: it is better to combine a lot of eye-catching necklaces with less conspicuous earrings, while eye-catching earrings should be combined with more discreet necklaces.

Creoles, plugs and earcuffs can combine to create great earcandy looks that are truly extraordinary. Bracelets can also be super combined, as can rings. Here, too, the rule is: the focus is only on one of the two places. So wear either several rings or several bracelets on one hand, both can quickly look cluttered
